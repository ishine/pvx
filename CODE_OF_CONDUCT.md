# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to a welcoming, inclusive, and technically rigorous community.

Expected behavior:

- Be respectful and professional in discussion and review.
- Focus feedback on ideas, code, and evidence.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Accept and provide constructive criticism.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, intimidation, or sustained hostile behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to repository spaces, issue discussions, pull
requests, and any project communication channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block participation for
behavior that violates this policy.
